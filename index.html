Canterbury Clinic is a private, non-profit facility devoted to assisting chemically dependent individuals who are seeking a comprehensive approach to their problems.

We are gender and orientation neutral. We provide intake and initial clinical assessment and adapt treatment to the needs of individuals.


PEOPLE CAN often feel powerless in facing their drug use/abuse if added medical complications produce a sense that there is no chance of improved health.

Canterbury Clinic is funded through the government and private charitable donations.

As medical issues are addressed, the patient sees improved health and many of the potential triggers of chemical dependency are removed.
